Our Services

The HealthSteps platform provides mobile healthcare management, reminders, and communication for patients and healthcare providers, particularly for the providers to offer their patients and primary caregivers of their dependents (child, pets) a way to keep in contact and manage care after a visit.

For Patients

Get a post-visit step-by-step care card from your doctor on every crucial detail of your care. Reminders make sure you never miss critical doses or exercises and our HIPAA compliant data keeps your healthcare provider in the loop in real time, alerting them to any issues.

For Caregivers

Be confident your loved one is taking adhering with their care plan and easily share critical instructions with anyone you want in the loop from family members to healthcare providers.

For Medical Device Companies

We’re partnering with leading smart medical device manufacturers to integrate HealthSteps and the critical data points these new technologies give us to provide advanced analytics to healthcare partners.


For Healthcare Providers

Be certain patients understand and adhere to their care plans. HealthSteps customizes care plan rollout to your existing workflows and prioritizes the alerts, triggers, and data that are most critical for you to see in real-time. From custom integrations with clinical trials to reducing readmissions at hospitals and clinics, implementing your care plan through HealthSteps empowers you to deliver the information and reminders patients need in a way that’s easiest for them to understand and act on.

HealthSteps: Ultimate Dream

HealthSteps believes the best healthcare outcomes starts with the patient by providing immediate access to the instructions and information they need to get better faster. HealthSteps, LLC was formed to improve patient-provider interactions and follow-up. By using mobile devices and web application, patients and their caregivers are able to manage their care plan and symptom tracking after a healthcare provider visit. HealthSteps also addresses the problems in long-term medical issues like diabetes and heart disease, particularly in terms of following their daily care plan, tracking symptoms, and providing accountability to family members with notifications.

HealthSteps will be a critical platform in the Patient Engagement space, expected to be an industry in the tens of billions in revenue by the early 2020s.

Goals

Deliver exceptional service to patients that puts them in the driver’s seat of their healthcare.

Bring our passion for improving healthcare to each interaction with every stakeholder.

Make customer investments in our technology revenue positive with reduced healthcare costs.

Improve the intelligence of healthcare by gathering and interpreting the best data sources.
